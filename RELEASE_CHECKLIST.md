- [ ] `LICENSE` exists
- [ ] `README.md` is current
- [ ] `ARCHITECTURE.md` exists
- [ ] `CONTRIBUTING.md` exists
- [ ] `SECURITY.md` exists
- [ ] `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` exists

- [ ] No raw secrets are exposed in UI fields
